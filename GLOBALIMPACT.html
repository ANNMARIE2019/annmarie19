Bless-A-Child

War, poverty, disease, whatever the cause, its tragic when a child loses one or both parents. many orphans end up on the streets where they often starve, get sick and suffer intolerable abuse. Through feeding programs,education, health care and more,Annmarie foundation....bless a child program is reaching out to those precious orphans and other vulnerable children, giving them hope for a better future.
