VOLUNTEER

Would you or some one you know like to volunteer when major landslides and other devasting storms strike Uganda and other countries where our foundation is operating?

Volunteers become a key component of Annmarie Foundation disaster relief efforts by serving hot meals and handing out bags of relief to help residents. AMFI-Volunteers are critical to bringing relief in real time.

AMFI will be accepting volunteers daily at 8:00am(Mon-Sat)

AMFI will provide everything you need from work assignments,work tools and allowances like transport, housing and meals.
