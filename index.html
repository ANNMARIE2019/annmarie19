ORPHANS & VULNERABLE CHILDREN

One of Annmarie Foundation core mission is to help orphaned children as well as those who are in vulnerable situations at home or at school.Our mandate comes straight from the bible, which reminds us to reach out to Orphans in need.

"Religion that God our Father accepts as pure and faultless is to love and look after orphans and widows in their distress....james1:27"

about annmarie foundation programs & vulnerable children
Annmarie foundation intl has a variety of programs in many areas of uganda, helping orphans everyday. one of the most powerful way to help orphans and vulnerable children is to ensure that they have the opportunity to attend school.

around the world and uganda at large, annmarie foundation programs in partnership with other orphan care organisations are ensuring that these young people get education they need to get ahead.

another way, annmarie foundation supporters help orphaned kids and those who are struglling is through our community, school nutrition programs in countries like burundi, south sudan,somalia, ethiopia, india e.t.c

in africa, annmarie foundation partners with a variety of local organisations that help orphans and vulnerable children get the care they need. one example is staff reached out to 11yr old with a life changing aid.
