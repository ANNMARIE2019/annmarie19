ABOUT ANNMARIE FOUNDATION INTERNATIONAL

AMFI is a non-profit humanitarian organisation dedicated to demonstrating God's love by alleviating human need and suffering in Uganda and around the world.

Along with our faithful supporters, we not only provide physical relief to those in immediate need but also, we equip them with new knowledge to create sustainable solutions to combat poverty.

AMFI along with its its partners are devoted to being GOD'S HAND AND FEET in the world.
